Valve, if you read this then I urge you to please...look into the match making servers of this game, update your anti-cheat, something. Many of the members of this community are asking you to just do something against the bots when all solutions have been nothing but negatives on the game and some people may or already have jumped ship. I already jumped ship because I was tired of waiting for any sort of response to the whole situation with the bots, that its better to play something fun than wait for something only to expect false promises.
To those who read this review, if you want to play this game still despite my thoughts on this I urge you to play on community servers. The casual rank does not matter in the slightest, trust me. But as I stated before..I advise staying away from this game. Too much neglect from the devs and radio silence from said devs has made this game unplayable in official Valve matchmaking.
If the situation changes regarding this game, my review will be changed. Until then stay far away from this one.
